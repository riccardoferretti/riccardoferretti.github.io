We are hiring a senior software engineer and a product designer.

Senior Software Engineer

We are a small, ambitious team building the future of interface design based in London and SF.

  • You'll be the design lead for our interface design application. You'll shape its direction, figure out how to make it better
  • you'll translate complex functionality (data bindings! animation! multi-touch concurrent interaction!) into powerful, easy-to-use interface designs
  • You'll develop and test new interaction patterns
  • You'll use the whole spectrum of UX methods to improve our product. User interviews, participatory design, paper-based exploration, framer, pixate - whatever it takes
  • You'll work closely and unusually collaboratively with high-calibre engineers to bring your ideas to life.
  • You enjoy architecting a solution, executing it visually, and testing it empirically

You'll like it here if...

  • you enjoy experimenting with new technologies, including code
  • you're using products like framer, and dabble in things like canvas animations or processing
  • you love exploring boundaries - between design and interaction, art and music, gestures and sound, etc
  • you're in the zone when you're breaking down complex, abstract problems into concrete solutions that get people excited
  • you're happy to discuss this, have opinions on this, and are curious to figure out how this was built.
